3 Idiots is a comedy-drama film that follows the lives of three engineering students. The movie highlights the importance of friendship, creativity, and pursuing one's passion instead of blindly following societal expectations.
Main actors include Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani.
